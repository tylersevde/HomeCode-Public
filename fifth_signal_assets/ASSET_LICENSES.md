# The Fifth Signal Beta - texture sources

The runtime files in this directory are compact derivatives of the assets
listed below. Every source is released under Creative Commons Zero (CC0 1.0),
so attribution is not required and commercial use, modification, and
redistribution with the game are permitted. Source credit is retained here for
provenance and appreciation.

## Procedural audio

Beta III does not ship or imitate any third-party meme recording. Its four NPC
chip voices, idle motifs, action barks, elimination tones, and victory stings
are original PCM synthesized at runtime by `fifth_signal_cyoa.py` from pulse,
triangle, saw, overtone, and deterministic noise generators. They therefore
require no external audio license or attribution.
